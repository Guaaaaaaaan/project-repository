Gennshin Impact

Genshin Impact takes place in the fantasy world of Teyvat, which is home to seven distinct nations, each of which is tied to a different element and ruled by a different god. The story follows the Traveler, who has traveled across countless worlds with their twin sibling before becoming separated in Teyvat. The Traveler travels in search of the lost sibling with their companion Paimon and becomes involved in the affairs of Teyvat's nations.

Gameplay

The game type is an action role-playing game, and more than 30 characters can be unlocked by completing the task and wishing ( card drawing ) system. In addition, after the player reaches the 16th level of the adventure level, the online can be turned on, and up to four players can play the game together in the online mode.

Combat System

Players can select four characters at a time (a total of four characters for all players in online mode) to participate in the battle, and can switch quickly during the battle. Each character has two skills, elemental combat skills and elemental burst. Elemental combat skills can be activated as long as the cooldown of the moves expires. The casting of elemental bursts requires accumulation of elemental energy, and they are all cast in the form of elements. In addition, for characters such as Amber and Gan Yu who use bows as weapons, there is an aiming mode that is used as a separate attack method, and the normal attacks of these characters are automatic aiming, but the damage is weak.
