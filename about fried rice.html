Fried rice

Fried rice is rice cooked in a wok or frying pan , usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood or meat.

The common egg fried rice is a Maillard reaction . When the egg liquid and the surface of the rice are heated, a caramelization reaction occurs, and the surface of the rice grains will become hard and easy to separate. Refrigerate cooked rice.
